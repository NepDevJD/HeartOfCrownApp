Twin Princesses

Lain and Shion

The twins, Lain and Shion, currently rule Elsia, the exceptionally autonomous state in the Empire. In Elsia, magic and fortune telling has always been the object of study, and politics are heavily influenced by this. The royalty are always powerful magicians, and the 16ths rulers Lain and Shion are no exception; in fact, they are said to be the most powerful in Elsia's history. Everything in their state is decided by reading the stars, so naturally their candidacy for the throne was also a result of this.
